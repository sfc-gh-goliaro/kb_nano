### Cross-agent inconsistency resolved

**AutoBackbone (`load_backbone`) treatment** — two agents disagreed:
- Cross-verifier round 1 (chmv2, conditional_detr): "downgrade_to_partial — pure conv2d + interpolate + linear; no CUDA kernel needed; composable from L1"
- Slice 6 verifier (tvp, zoedepth): "demote_to_unsupported — kb-nano lacks the AutoBackbone abstraction"

**My resolution: partial.** Reasoning:
- AutoBackbone is HF's runtime mechanism for swapping backbone configs.
- The actual COMPUTE within any chosen backbone is composable from kb-nano L1 (conv2d, batch_norm2d, relu, etc.).
- "Missing AutoBackbone abstraction" is an infrastructure gap, not a primitive gap.
- For consistency with the v5 fixes (`detr`, `dab_detr`, `deformable_detr`, `prompt_depth_anything` all moved to partial because of `load_backbone`), I apply the same rule here.

## Slices 2-6 cross-slice consistency resolution (118 folder-touches)

Slices 2, 3, 4, 5 returned with verdicts that DISAGREED on three recurring patterns. I personally read source for the disputed cases and applied a consistent rule across all of them.

### Disagreement 1: `load_backbone` (AutoBackbone) treatment

**Conflicting agent verdicts:**
- Cross-verifier round 1 (chmv2, conditional_detr): **partial** ("no CUDA kernel needed; composable from L1")
- Slice 2 (chmv2, conditional_detr, dab_detr, detr, deformable_detr): **demote_to_unsupported** ("kb-nano lacks AutoBackbone abstraction")
- Slice 4 (omdet_turbo, oneformer): **demote_to_unsupported** (same)
- Slice 6 (tvp, zoedepth): **demote_to_unsupported** (same)

**My resolution: partial** for all 9 folders.

Reasoning (file:line evidence):
- `chmv2/modular_chmv2.py:23,76` — uses `consolidate_backbone_kwargs_to_config` and `backbone_config: AutoConfig`
- `detr/modeling_detr.py:261` — `backbone = load_backbone(config)`
- `tvp/modeling_tvp.py:138`, `zoedepth/modeling_zoedepth.py:1229` — same pattern
- All inner backbone compute (conv2d, batch_norm2d, relu) IS present in kb-nano L1.
- `load_backbone(config)` is HF runtime-routing infrastructure, not a missing compute primitive.
- Treating it as "unsupported" would require the same call for `detr`, `dab_detr`, `deformable_detr`, `prompt_depth_anything` (which v5 already moved to partial). For consistency, all 9 stay partial.

### Self-criticism

Slice 8 only caught 7 of the 13 genuine interleaved-RoPE folders (those that fell in its 40-folder random sample). Without my follow-up scan + source-read of the remaining candidates, the 6 additional cases (`gptj`, `cohere`, `codegen`, `glm4v`, `glm4`, `ernie4_5_vl_moe`) would have stayed mismarked as composable. I almost missed this — only the user's pushback ("did you take shortcuts?") prompted me to go back and verify the candidates.
